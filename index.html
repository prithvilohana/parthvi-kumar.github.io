Echo: A Music Web App

Echo is a minimalist and efficient music web app designed using JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and Bootstrap. Developed in 6 days, Echo dynamically populates the music library by fetching songs directly from your directory using the Fetch API. Without the need for a backend, Echo automatically lists and plays songs that are added to the specified folders. This lightweight and responsive app ensures a smooth and enjoyable listening experience across all devices, making it easy to manage and play your music collection.
